Inheritance mode values are candidate labels based on genotype assignments.

This case is labeled as proband-only. Since no other family members are in the analysis, most of the traditional inheritance modes can not be assigned.

Inheritance modes that can not be assigned on this case include:

  • de novo calls
  • Dominant (Maternal) vs Dominant (Paternal) vs Recessive
  • Loss of Heterozygosity

Variants on the X or Y chromosome are classified X-linked or Y-linked.

Low relevance:

  • Variants with missing calls (./.) or calls that mismatch the sex (e.g. 0/1 on chrX for a male) are not assigned inheritance mode values.
  • Variants with genotypes that do not meet any of the high relevance criteria above are not assigned inheritance mode labels.
