SIFT score predicts whether an amino acid substitution will affect protein function. Applicable to missense variants only. Scores range from 0-1, and a score of <0.05 indicates intolerance or deleteriousness. SIFT predictions are either "D" for damaging or "T" for tolerated. The rankscore is a value between 0-1 indicating where the SIFT score falls compared to all other variants with SIFT scores. For more information see the SIFT site or Ng et al (2003).
