Confidence classes are calculated according to fixed filters.

The relevant filters for Structural Variants (SVs) called by Manta include:

  • length : The length of the call
  • split-reads : The number of split reads supporting the call
  • split-read-ratio : The number of split reads supporting the call divided by the number of split reads supporting the reference
  • spanning-reads : The number of spanning reads supporting the call
  • spanning-read-ratio : The number of spanning reads supporting the call divided by the number of spanning reads supporting the reference

The relevant filters for Copy Number Variants (CNVs) called by BIC-seq2 include:

  • length : The length of the call
  • log-ratio : log2(observed reads/expected reads)

High Confidence Calls
  • SVs called by Manta:
    • length>250 bp & split-reads>5 & split-read-ratio>0.3 & spanning-reads>5 & spanning-read-ratio>0.3
    • length<250 bp & split-reads>5 & split-read-ratio>0.3
  • CNVs called by BIC-seq2:
    • length>1 Mbp & 0.4<log-ratio<0.8
Medium Confidence Calls
  • SVs called by Manta:
    • length>250 bp & split-reads>3 & split-read-ratio>0.3 & spanning-reads>3 & spanning-read-ratio>0.3
    • length<250 bp & split-reads>3 & split-read-ratio>0.3
Low Confidence Calls
  • All other variants
